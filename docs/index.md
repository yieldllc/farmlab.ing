# Welcome to Farmlab.ing

Your comprehensive resource for small-scale farming documentation, guides, and best practices.

## What is a Farmlab?

A farmlab is your personal agricultural experimentation space where you can explore sustainable farming techniques, test new growing methods, and develop self-sufficient food production systems.

## Getting Started

New to small-scale farming? Check out our [Getting Started Guide](getting-started.md) to begin your journey.

## Features

- **Growing Guides** - Learn to grow your own food year-round
- **Soil & Composting** - Build healthy, productive soil
- **Irrigation Systems** - Efficient water management
- **Livestock Basics** - Small-scale animal husbandry
